3. Escrow Transactions

3.1 How Escrow Works

Our escrow system protects both buyers and sellers:

  1. Seller Creates Escrow: Seller creates an escrow transaction with item details and price
  2. Buyer Pays: Buyer sends cryptocurrency payment, which is held in secure escrow
  3. Seller Delivers: Seller delivers the item or service to the buyer
  4. Buyer Confirms: Buyer confirms receipt of the item
  5. Automatic Release: Funds are automatically released to the seller upon confirmation

3.2 Escrow Rules

  • Funds are held in escrow until both parties confirm the transaction is complete
  • Escrow transactions have a timeout period (configurable by seller)
  • If buyer does not confirm receipt, seller can request admin review after timeout
  • Cryptocurrency escrow addresses are unique and generated per transaction
  • We do not have access to escrow private keys without multi-signature approval
