Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 7, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Lockbay is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our peer-to-peer marketplace with cryptocurrency escrow services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Telegram user ID, username, and profile information
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number (for verification and notifications)
  • Transaction Information: Details about trades, escrow transactions, payment amounts, and cryptocurrency addresses
  • Banking Information: Bank account details for fiat withdrawals (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Communications: Messages exchanged through our platform for dispute resolution and support

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Platform interactions, feature usage, and transaction history
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type
  • Transaction Metadata: Timestamps, transaction status, and payment confirmations
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies for authentication and platform functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Platform Operations: To operate, maintain, and improve our peer-to-peer marketplace and escrow services
  • Transaction Processing: To facilitate cryptocurrency escrow transactions, payment processing, and fund releases
  • User Verification: To verify user identity and prevent fraud, money laundering, and other illegal activities
  • Notifications: To send transaction updates, escrow status changes, and important platform announcements via email, SMS, and Telegram
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, resolve disputes, and provide technical assistance
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and investigate fraudulent transactions and security threats
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, including anti-money laundering (AML) and know-your-customer (KYC) requirements
  • Analytics: To analyze platform usage, improve user experience, and develop new features

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted using 256-bit AES encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access controls
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control (RBAC) limits employee access to user data
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and vulnerability assessments are conducted regularly
  • Cryptocurrency Security: Private keys for escrow wallets are stored in cold storage with multi-signature protection

3.2 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Transaction records are kept for a minimum of 7 years for regulatory compliance.

4. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party service providers to deliver our services:

  • Telegram: Our platform integrates with Telegram for user authentication and messaging
  • Cryptocurrency Payment Processors: For cryptocurrency payment processing and wallet services
  • Email Service: For transactional emails and notifications
  • SMS Service: For SMS verification and notifications
  • Banking Partners: For fiat currency withdrawals and payment processing
  • Cloud Hosting: Secure cloud infrastructure for platform hosting and data storage

These third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session and authentication state
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security

You can control cookie settings through your browser, but disabling cookies may affect platform functionality.

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities, such as analytics

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

8. Children's Privacy
